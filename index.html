Our facility

Range, technology, and short game

Range, Toptracer, club fitting with TrackMan, club repair, and mini golf—each has a dedicated page in the header. Lessons are on the lessons page.

Premium mats & grass tees

Thirty-six artificial-grass bays and fifteen grass bays, with mats replaced each season and divots tended daily. Targets span roughly 25 yards out to 200 and beyond.

Full range details

Toptracer ball-tracking

Every shot into the range can be tracked for practice and play modes—including virtual golf with friends. Sessions are not clock-based, so you can work at your own pace.

Toptracer modes

Miniature golf & groups

A memorable mini-golf layout with water in play on nearly every hole. Range ends include couches, Toptracer, and large TVs—each bay fits up to about four guests comfortably.

Hours & Policies

Weekly hours and visit guidelines

Gastrau's Golf Center operates year-round in Oak Creek. Hours below reflect our public posted schedule; outdoor areas may vary with weather.

Facility hours

  • Monday: 10 am – 9 pm
  • Tuesday – Friday: 10 am – 10 pm
  • Saturday: 9 am – 10 pm
  • Sunday: 9 am – 9 pm
  • Miniature golf: 9 am – 10 pm daily (posted on site)

Range & practice

  • Heated hitting options support winter practice; confirm seasonal setup when you call.
  • Finish balls and return rental equipment by closing or as directed by staff.
  • Follow all posted safety rules in bays and on walkways near active flight.
  • Toptracer bays are ideal for small groups—plan extra time for larger parties.

Events & group outings

  • Birthday inquiries: contact birthday coordinator Katie Gastrau (see email below).
  • Corporate outings, field trips, fundraisers: use the form or call the main line.
  • Large groups should confirm date, headcount, and timing before arrival.
  • Beverages and food options vary; ask staff what is available during your visit.

Guest conduct

  • Children and teens should be supervised by a responsible adult.
  • Respect staff directions and other guests; keep pace where bays are shared.
  • Mini-golf putters must be used as intended—misuse may incur a replacement fee.
  • Questions about weather or bay availability? Call before you make the trip.

Our story

Milwaukee-area golf under one roof

Founded in 1998 as a focused practice facility, Gastrau's Golf Center grew into a year-round destination with heated bays, club building and repair, retail, and technology like Toptracer—still rooted in hands-on service for every golfer.

Steve Gastrau

Owner and certified club builder; leads the shop's technical standards and long-term vision.

Brent Gastrau

General manager; day-to-day operations, guest experience, and staff coordination.

Katie Gastrau

Birthday and events coordinator; helps families and groups plan celebrations and outings.

Practice and play

From premium range balls and maintained tees to Toptracer games and virtual rounds, the facility is designed so practice feels engaging—not rushed—and translates to better golf on the course.

Fit, repair, shop

Custom fitting, re-gripping, reshafting, and equipment sales support players who want gear matched to their swing. Ask in the shop for TrackMan, demo days, and lesson options.
