Heated driving range

Real ball flight outdoors, serious warmth from infrared heaters, and Wisconsin-proof practice all winter long.

Winter practice

Hit comfortably outside

Golf is hard, especially if you are taking four months off every winter. Why stop if you can hit comfortably outside?

Our goal throughout winter is to give you the most comfortable and realistic practice during the off-season. That means fewer layers while still seeing real ball flight outdoors underneath our toasty heaters.

We have individual infrared heaters pushing out enough heat to keep you warm even in frigid Wisco temperatures down into the single digits.

Not to mention, we have back heaters that keep golfers waiting to hit warm and cozy.

Always hit in comfort.

Toptracer · Ball-tracking · Technology

Feedback you can trust

"An extremely versatile system that allows for anything from statistical analysis to playing a round of golf."

Toptracer allows for each shot hit to be traced with extremely accurate feedback within a yard of actual flight.

Use it to figure out the strengths and weaknesses of your game, play rounds of golf with friends, or even figure out the yardages of your bag.

The best part is that there is no time limit to finish, unlike most simulators.

Premium range balls & artificial turf

With over 80,000 premium TaylorMade range balls, ball flight and distances translate to the course. No limited flight, so you can trust the results.

Our artificial hitting mats are replaced yearly as well, so you are hitting off the most realistic and shock-absorptive surface we can offer.
