Club repair

Fast turnaround regrips, reshafting, lie and loft, length changes, and more—ask the shop if your repair is not listed.

Services

What we offer

Club re-gripping

Your clubs are treated with care—typically 24-hour turnaround for fourteen clubs or fewer.

  • Brands: Golf Pride, Winn, SuperStroke, Lamkin
  • Install: $3 per club (+$1 per club if grips are purchased elsewhere)

Reshafting

Broken shaft or a swing change? The shop can match you to an in-stock or custom-order shaft.

  • Brands: Fujikura, KBS, True Temper (other brands via custom order)
  • Turnaround: about 2 days for in-stock shafts
  • Pricing: depends on shaft and clubhead—quote in shop

Lie & loft adjustment

Wrong lie makes solid contact hard to repeat. Not every iron can be bent—forged models usually can; some cast heads cannot. Bring brand and model and staff will verify.

  • Turnaround: about 1 day (putters may take longer)
  • Cost: $5 per club

Length adjustment

Complimentary ~10-minute consultation to recommend length, then precise shortening or extension.

  • Turnaround: 1–2 days
  • Shorten: $4 per club · Lengthen: $6 per club

Miscellaneous

  • Save grip: $4 per club (Winn, JumboMax, and Star grips excluded)
  • Reset club: $15 per club
  • Remove rattle: $15

If a repair is not listed, call or email and the team will confirm whether it can be done.

The official site also lists a short club repair consultation appointment type—use it for questions before you drop off a full bag of work.
