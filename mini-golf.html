Miniature golf

Open 9am–10pm daily

Gastrau's mini golf is for beginners and scratch players alike—water comes into play on nearly every hole, so strategy and touch both matter.

Whether it is a family outing or a friendly wager with friends, the course is built to be memorable. Per-player pricing is available at the register on site.

Happy putting!

Putter policy: Anyone who breaks a putter through misuse will be charged $15 to replace the damaged equipment.

Pair mini golf with the driving range or Toptracer for a full day at the facility.

Birthday parties

Want to celebrate your birthday with mini golf, relay races, and so much more? Then Gastrau's is the place for you!!

Inquire now!

The details

Parties are about two hours in length and run by two employees who we like to call “party heroes,” so parents can sit back and relax. Unlike many other kids' parties, we let kids choose from different games and relays throughout the day. We take care of everything—food, drinks, activities, and even birthday invitations.

Activities & food

Activities include:

  • Round of mini golf
  • Marshmallow golf
  • Relay races
  • Parachute games
  • Choice of pizza, cake, and drinks
  • Treat bags for all guests in attendance

Booking details

  • All parties must be scheduled two weeks in advance.
  • A $50 deposit is required.
  • Deposits are refunded only if the party is cancelled eight or more days before the scheduled date.
  • The cost is $250 for the first eight kids and $10 for each additional child.
